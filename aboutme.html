About Me

My full name is Eric Minseo Park, but call me Min. I'm currently 18, working a full time job as a cook. On my spare time I often practice my technical skills in Super Smash Bros. Melee, practice hip hop choreography, or code and design projects like this. I do occasionally binge on youtube or anime, but now I'm keeping to my new years resolution to set my priorities straight!

I dropped out of The University of Waterloo's Honors Mathematics program after realizing the career path wasn't for me. I decided to invest my current time in training my fundamental abilities to be completely prepared for a career in programming. This includes time management, critical thinking, practical ability (coding), and judgement.

I am now setting my sights on The University of Guelph's Honors Software Engineering program, The University of Toronto's Honors Computer Science program, and The University of Western Ontario's Honors Computer Science program.
